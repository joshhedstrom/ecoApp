Purpose of App: 

To create and facilitate global initiatives to combat climate change_history

Aspects:

Lists of ways to individually reduce carbon emissions and energy consumption
Ways to start petitions for initiatives, specific to country
Notifications of online deals and coupons for energy friendly products
Partnerships with energy friendly and eco innovative startups and companies
Funding for cheap alternative energy products
Tracking and analytics on user's environmentally friendly activities (volunteered info)
Community Forums
